Advantages of Society Formation:

  • F.S.I granted by change of Development Regulations.
  • Society formation helps member to plan events, maintenance or meetings more effectively, as committee gets the authority.
  • Society can send a legal notice to builder for doing the conveyance deed, to which builder has to reply.
  • Society formation can help society to avoid builder taking advantage of additional
  • By this Society can issue NOC to members when they are selling their flats.

Advantages of Conveyance Deed:

  • Society will get Proper and Legal Title of the Property (Land & Structures) in the name of the Society on 7/12 (Satbara Extract)
  • Society will retain Additional F.S.I granted by change of Development Regulations
  • Society can get permission for reconstruction from the Planning Authorities
  • Society can Redevelop the Property by constructing new building using TDR & Members can get the additional area (F.S.I), new amenities, etc.
